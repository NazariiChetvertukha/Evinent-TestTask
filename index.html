× This is a simple web application that allows users to select four points on the canvas. Based on these points, two circles are drawn, and the intersections of these circles are calculated and displayed.

Instructions:
  • Click on the canvas to select four points.
  • The first two points will define the center and radius of the first circle.
  • The next two points will define the center and radius of the second circle.
  • Intersections of the two circles will be calculated and displayed.
  • You can drag the points to move them and see the updated circles and intersections.
  • Use the "Reset" button to clear the canvas and start over.
  • Use the "About" button to see this information.
